How to play
  1. Choose your formation, tactical style, and mode (Classic shows ratings, Almanac hides them).
  2. Each roll deals one real national squad (country + World Cup year). Pick one player and tap a glowing slot to place him.
  3. A new squad is dealt for the next slot. Eleven rolls fill the whole XI.
  4. Don't like the squad you rolled? Use a swap to change the country or year — 3 swaps per run, and they don't cost you a pick.
  5. Slots only accept compatible positions. Out-of-position players (~) lose rating.
  6. Fill all 11, then Simulate: 3 group games + Round of 16, QF, SF, Final.
  7. Win all seven and you close it 7–0 — the rarest, most shareable result.
