How to play 7–0

Build a World Cup dream team by drafting real legends from across every era, then take them through a seven-match knockout run. Win all seven unbeaten and you close it 7–0 — a flawless, perfectly-fitted side and the rarest, most shareable result in the game.

  1. 1
    Set up. Pick a formation, a style (Defensive / Balanced / Attacking) and a mode — Classic shows ratings, Almanac hides them for a pure memory test.
  2. 2
    Draft your XI — eleven rolls. Each roll deals one real national squad (e.g. Brazil 1970). Tap one player, then tap a glowing slot to place him. A fresh squad is dealt for the next slot.
  3. 3
    Swaps. Don't like the squad you rolled? Spend a swap to change the country or year. You get 3 per run and they never cost you a pick.
  4. 4
    Position fit. Slots only accept compatible roles. A player out of his natural position is marked ~ and loses rating — a #10 can't play full-back, and only a keeper goes in goal.
  5. 5
    Simulate. With all 11 placed, hit Simulate and watch every match play out live — 3 group games, then Round of 16, Quarter-final, Semi-final and the Final.
  6. 6
    Close it 7–0. Win all seven in normal time for the perfect run. Knockouts won on penalties still advance you but break the 7–0. Your perfect runs, streak and best are saved on this device.
💡 Lock in your goalkeeper and defence early — clean sheets win knockouts.
